vitamin c

Kiwi fruit, or Chinese gooseberry, is a nutrient-rich berry with fuzzy brown skin and vibrant green or golden flesh. It’s packed with vitamin C, fiber, and antioxidants. Native to China, it’s now widely grown in New Zealand and beyond, offering a tangy-sweet flavor.

Pomegranates

Pomegranates are nutrient-rich fruits with a tough red rind and juicy, seed-filled arils. They're packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and anti-inflammatory properties, benefiting heart health, digestion, and immunity. Used in juices, dishes, and medicine.
